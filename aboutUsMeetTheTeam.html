Dr. Chiyo Shidara D.D.S

Dr. Shidara was born and raised in Tokyo, Japan and graduated from Nippon Dental University. She moved to California with her family shortly after receiving her Dental Degree to pursue a dental career in the Bay Area. She obtained her California Dental License from the University of California, San Francisco and the University of the Pacific where she served as an associate instructor for 4 years. She was also appointed department head at the Contra Costa County Hospital and supervised the Dental Department for 5 years. With over 20 years of experience, she says what motivates her is being able to interact with patients everyday alongside her talented staff. In her spare time, she is an avid runner, singer, and a mother of 3.
