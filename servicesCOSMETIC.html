Cosmetic Dental Services

Invisalign

In addition to correcting crooked teeth, Invisalign can improve bite functions, preventing further oral health issues such as cavities, gum infections, and erosion of tooth enamel amongst other things. We offer a complimentary consultation for interested patients.

Veneers

There are many advantages to veneering. In addition to covering chipped or stained teeth, veneers can restore and strengthen worn enamel. We use biocompatible material to create personalized veneers for each of our patients and ensure comfort during and after the procedure.

Teeth Whitening

We utilize several different teeth whitening procedures depedning on our patients' preferences. Please consult with Dr. Shidara and staff if you are interested.
